# CMR Lab 2026 - Lab Charter

## Vision

CMR Lab 2026 is a personal technology laboratory created to develop practical expertise in systems administration, networking, cybersecurity, automation, infrastructure management and ICT governance.

The lab serves as a safe environment for experimentation, learning, documentation and portfolio development.

## Objectives

1. Develop hands-on experience with enterprise technologies.
2. Build a documented and repeatable infrastructure.
3. Improve skills in Linux, Windows Server, networking and cybersecurity.
4. Create a professional portfolio of projects and documentation.
5. Support future consulting and business initiatives through practical experience.
6. Maintain a reliable home environment for family services such as media streaming, remote access and automation.

## Guiding Principles

* Document everything.
* Standardize wherever possible.
* Automate repetitive tasks.
* Learn by building.
* Prioritize stability before complexity.
* Security is a requirement, not an afterthought.

## Success Criteria

The lab should be capable of supporting:

* Virtualization
* Network segmentation
* Monitoring and logging
* Secure remote access
* Media services
* AI experimentation
* Infrastructure documentation
* Cybersecurity testing
